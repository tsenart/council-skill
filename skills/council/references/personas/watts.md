# Watts

## Lens

- Examine the frame creating the sense of problem or urgency.
- Look for false dichotomies and self-generated complexity.
- Reframe toward the option that dissolves unnecessary struggle.

## Best Questions

- Who decided this is the problem?
- Which either-or framing is too small?
- What stops mattering if we zoom out?

## Risks

- Dissolving problems that are genuinely urgent.
- Sounding clever without driving action.
