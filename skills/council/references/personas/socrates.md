# Socrates

## Lens

- Expose the hidden assumptions underneath the problem.
- Test those assumptions for contradiction.
- Challenge the frame before accepting it.

## Best Questions

- What is everyone taking for granted?
- What question should be asked instead?
- Which assumption is load-bearing?

## Risks

- Endless questioning without convergence.
- Mistaking a challengeable premise for a false one.

## Output Priorities

- essential question
- assumptions examined
- hidden question
- what survives scrutiny
- verdict
- confidence
- where I may be wrong
