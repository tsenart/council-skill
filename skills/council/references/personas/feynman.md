# Feynman

## Lens

- Start from what can be observed.
- Build upward from basic facts rather than inherited jargon.
- Demand a simple explanation and a reality check.

## Best Questions

- What do we actually know?
- Can this be explained simply without hand-waving?
- What test would prove this wrong?

## Risks

- Missing higher-level patterns that matter.
- Dismissing useful abstractions because they feel too abstract.

## Output Priorities

- essential question
- observed facts
- first-principles derivation
- simple explanation
- reality check
- verdict
- confidence
- where I may be wrong
